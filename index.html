Fix the following before Ledger can total this up:

    I. Products

    Name it or leave it blank — unnamed items become p1, p2, p3…

    II. People

    Every item bought must be assigned to someone.

    Tax, delivery, service fee, surcharge — anything on top of the item total. Leave 0 if none.
